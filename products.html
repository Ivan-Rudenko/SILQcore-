Quartz Sand Products

High-purity quartz sand for glass, construction, and industrial applications across Ukraine. Explore our range of fractions optimized for your production needs.

Our Product Fractions

Each fraction of quartz sand is carefully processed to meet specific industry requirements, ensuring high quality, consistency, and optimal performance for your projects.

Quartz Sand 0.1–0.4 mm

Finest fraction, ideal for high-quality glass production, fiberglass, and optical applications. Guarantees uniform melting and extremely low impurities.

  • Purity: ≥ 99%
  • Moisture: ≤ 0.2%
  • Applications: Flat glass, optical glass, fiberglass

Quartz Sand 0.4–0.8 mm

Medium fraction suitable for construction and industrial casting. Provides excellent compaction, stability, and consistency in concrete and mortar mixes.

  • Purity: ≥ 98%
  • Moisture: ≤ 0.3%
  • Applications: Concrete, mortar, industrial casting

Quartz Sand 0.8–1.2 mm

Coarse fraction designed for high-strength concrete, paving, and sandblasting. Ensures excellent drainage and structural durability.

  • Purity: ≥ 97%
  • Moisture: ≤ 0.3%
  • Applications: Concrete, paving, filtration, sandblasting

Quartz Sand 1.2–1.6 mm

Extra coarse fraction for heavy-duty concrete, decorative surfaces, and industrial applications. Provides high mechanical strength, permeability, and durability.

  • Purity: ≥ 96%
  • Moisture: ≤ 0.3%
  • Applications: Heavy-duty concrete, decorative surfaces, sandblasting
