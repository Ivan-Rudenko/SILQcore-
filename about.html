About SilQcore

SilQcore is a specialized company focused on the extraction and industrial enrichment of high-purity quartz sand for key sectors of modern industry.

Our Approach

We build our work on a combination of technological expertise, strict quality control, and a responsible approach to natural resources.

SilQcore ensures predictable raw material quality, stable supply volumes, and flexibility to meet customer needs.

Mission

To supply the market with quartz raw materials that meet international standards, creating sustainable industrial value and long-term partnerships.
